  - **Net change**: **−81 lines**, eliminates copy-paste drift risk across 23 files

#### HEAD Resolution Consolidation — `commit/head.zig` (M2)

- **Consolidated 10 duplicate `resolveHead*()` wrappers** into canonical [`head_mod.resolveHeadOid()`](src/commit/head.zig) and new [`head_mod.readHeadRef()`](src/commit/head.zig):
  - Removed wrapper methods from `blame/blame.zig`, `describe/describe.zig` (×2), `cli/archive.zig`, `cli/commit.zig`, `cli/filter_repo.zig`, `cli/format_patch.zig`, `cli/log.zig`, `cli/name_rev.zig`, `cli/rev_list.zig`
  - Removed **25-line full duplicate implementation** from `checkout/restore.zig`
  - Added `readHeadRef()` helper for symbolic ref path resolution (e.g., `"refs/heads/main"`)
  - Fixed argument order mismatch in `name_rev.zig` (`allocator, io` → `io, allocator`)
  - **Net change**: **−56 lines**
